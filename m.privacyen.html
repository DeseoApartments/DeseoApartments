PRIVACY AND COOKIE POLICY

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. Our website does not collect personal information through cookies or third-party tracking tools. The cookies we use are strictly necessary for the proper functioning of the site.

2. Types of Data Collected

We do not collect automated personal data through profiling cookies or tracking tools. The personal data we may collect only includes the information you voluntarily provide through our contact forms (e.g., name, email, phone number).

3. Use of Data

We use the personal data collected through contact forms to respond to your requests and provide assistance. We do not use this data for marketing purposes, nor do we share it with third parties.

4. Necessary Cookies

Our website uses only technical cookies that are necessary to ensure the correct operation of the site and to improve your browsing experience. These cookies do not require your explicit consent and do not collect personal information.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or unauthorized disclosures. However, the site does not store sensitive or personal data through tracking tools.

6. How to Manage Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies directly from your browser settings. However, please note that disabling technical cookies may negatively affect the functionality of the site.

7. Contacts

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy or the use of cookies, feel free to contact us at the following email address: info@deseoapartments.com.
