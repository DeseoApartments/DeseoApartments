PRIVACY POLICY


Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are regarding your data.

1. Personal Data Collected

We collect various types of personal data to provide and improve our services. The data we collect includes:

  • Contact information, such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Usage data, such as how you interact with our site.
  • Payment information, where applicable.

2. How We Use Data

We use your personal data for various purposes, including:

  • To provide the requested services.
  • To improve user experience on our site.
  • To communicate with you about our services and promotions.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless strictly necessary to provide the requested services or required by law.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your personal data with appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, delete your personal data, or limit its processing. You can also object to the processing of your data for specific purposes and request data portability.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, feel free to contact us at: info@deseoapartments.com.
